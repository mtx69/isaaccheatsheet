What's this for?

This is a cheatsheet that you put behind your Binding of Isaac game so you can get a quick item reference while playing. See here.

Designed for Macs with a resolution of at least 1940x1080. (Sorry, I'll see what I can do for Windows :/).

Download
the Mac app.

Run it, hide the statusbar and maximize the screen.

Made with Fluid

Thank you reddit contributors (and whoever ripped those sprites and put them on mediafire)! Give all your monies to Ed McMillen and Florian Himsl.
